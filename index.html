Why OpenIntel
Built different
Most OSINT tools send your queries to someone else's servers. OpenIntel runs entirely on your machine — your targets, your data, your rules.
100% Self-Hosted
Runs on 127.0.0.1:7658. Zero telemetry. Your queries never leave your machine unless you initiate a lookup.
Telegram OTP Auth
No passwords stored anywhere. Register or sign in with a username — a Telegram bot delivers a one-time 6-digit code to your account.
Persistent History
Every lookup from every tool is logged to a local SQLite database tied to your account. Filter, search, and re-run anything with one click.
Intelligence Graph
A Maltego-style canvas to visually map connections between names, emails, IPs, wallets, and addresses. Right-click to add, link, and edit nodes.
Universal Search
Paste any input — IP, domain, email, username, phone, wallet. OpenIntel auto-detects the type and fires every relevant tool simultaneously.
Multi-Source Accuracy
Critical lookups cross-reference multiple APIs in parallel — 4 subdomain sources, 3 IP providers, 3 MAC vendors — for maximum coverage and accuracy.
